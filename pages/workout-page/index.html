Workout plans

Bulking workouts

For Bulking

Day 1 – Chest & Triceps: Bench Press, Incline Dumbbell Press, Cable Flys, Tricep Dips.
Day 2 – Back & Biceps: Deadlifts, Pull-ups, Barbell Rows, Bicep Curls.
Day 3 – Legs: Squats, Leg Press, Walking Lunges, Calf Raises.
Day 4 – Shoulders: Overhead Press, Lateral Raises, Arnold Press, Shrugs.
Day 5 – Full Body Strength: Squats, Deadlifts, Bench Press, Pull-ups.
Day 6 – Active Rest: Light cardio, stretching, or yoga.
Day 7 – Rest.

Lean Bulk workouts

For Lean Bulking

Day 1 – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps): Bench Press, Overhead Press, Dumbbell Flys, Skull Crushers.
Day 2 – Pull (Back, Biceps): Deadlifts, Pull-ups, Lat Pulldown, Hammer Curls.
Day 3 – Legs: Front Squats, Bulgarian Split Squats, Leg Extensions, Calf Raises.
Day 4 – Upper Strength: Incline Bench, Bent-over Rows, Pull-ups, Barbell Curls.
Day 5 – Lower Strength: Squats, Romanian Deadlifts, Hip Thrusts.
Day 6 – Core & Conditioning: Planks, Hanging Leg Raises, Farmer’s Carry, Sled Push.
Day 7 – Rest.

Weight Loss workout

For Weight Loss

Day 1 – Full Body Strength: Squats, Push-ups, Dumbbell Rows, Plank.
Day 2 – HIIT Cardio: Sprint Intervals, Burpees, Jump Squats, Mountain Climbers.
Day 3 – Upper Body Strength: Bench Press, Shoulder Press, Bicep Curls, Tricep Dips.
Day 4 – Lower Body Strength: Squats, Lunges, Deadlifts, Step-ups.
Day 5 – Circuit Training: Jump Rope, Kettlebell Swings, Push-ups, Sit-ups (repeat 3–4 rounds).
Day 6 – Active Cardio: Cycling, Swimming, or Running.
Day 7 – Rest.
