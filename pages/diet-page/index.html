Diet Plans

Bulking Diet

For Bulking

Eat carbs + protein 1–2 hrs before workout (chicken/paneer + rice or oats + banana).
Have a quick snack 30 mins before (banana or rice cakes).
Post-workout: whey protein + eggs/chicken + fruit.
Rest of the day: balanced meals with protein, rice/roti, veggies, and healthy fats.
Before bed: curd or paneer for slow-digesting protein.

Lean Bulk Diet

For Lean Bulking

Pre-workout (1–2 hrs): oats/sweet potato + chicken/paneer + veggies.
30 mins before: banana or black coffee for energy.
Post-workout: whey protein + eggs/chicken + fruit.
Day meals: clean carbs (rice, roti, quinoa), lean protein, veggies, healthy fats.
Before bed: paneer/curd + seeds/nuts for slow release protein.

Weight Loss Diet

For Weight Loss

Pre-workout (1–2 hrs): oats/boiled egg whites/fruit for light energy.
30 mins before: black coffee or green tea (optional).
Post-workout: whey protein + boiled eggs/chicken + salad.
Day meals: high protein, lots of veggies, moderate carbs (brown rice/roti), healthy fats.
Before bed: curd/Greek yogurt or paneer in small portion.
